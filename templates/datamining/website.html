Figshare is an online data knowledge base based on cloud computing technology. Researchers can save and share their research results, including data, data sets, images, videos, posters and codes, so that their research, even preliminary research, is exposed and recognized. Because figshare follows the principle of open data, users can freely access data and upload content. Therefore, researchers can disclose their data through figshare. All entries on figshare have DOI for easy reference
